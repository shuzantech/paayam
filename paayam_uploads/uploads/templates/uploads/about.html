About Us

AIPIF is a non-political, moral, ethical, and purely humanitarian organization serving humanity by spreading the message of love, brotherhood, and human values among humankind irrespective of caste, creed, or religion with the sole purpose of connecting person-to-person and heart-to-heart.

About The Founder

Maulana Abul Hasan Nadvi, often referred to as Ali Miyan, was an influential figure in the Islamic world.

He authored over fifty books in various languages, including "The Message of Humanity."

He was a renowned international Islamic scholar & was Vice Chancelor of Darul-Uloom Nadwat-ul-ulama.

Was awarded King Faisal award for the Humanitarian services

His efforts to bridge understanding between different religious communities have left a lasting legacy among the masses.

Aims & Objectives

1. To create mutual good-will and an atmosphere of affection kindness purely on human and national levels founded on BROTHERHOOD and WELL-BEING of MANKIND generally; to put an end to violence, lawlessness, anarchy, moral degradation and create FELLOW-FEELING towards each other.

2. To hold Public meetings and Seminars for promoting and spreading the sense of sharing sorrows and sufferings of human beings.

...

3. To weed-out from the society and human race: Bribery and illegal gratiﬁcation;Nepotism and favoritism;Misconceptions arising out of hatred;Hoarding, proﬁteering and black-marketing;Sectarianism;Disparity in economic and living standards and Full-ﬂedged struggle against sub-standards behavioral inclinations and obscenity.

4. To eradicate all ill-founded rites, rituals and cruel conventions.

5. To uplift the poor, the backward, the neglected, the down-trodden, and the agonized people irrespective of caste, creed, colour and gender.

6. To create a sense of Sobriety, social service, submissiveness, self-respect, self-restraint, self-conﬁdence, self-vigilance, and to raise cultural and intellectual levels of the STUDENT COMMUNITY and the up and coming GENERATION there by helping them to grow into better and useful citizens.

7. To extend all possible help in personal sphere and on the levels of al locality, village and town and ﬁnally, in the Country
